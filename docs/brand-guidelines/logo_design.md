# OpenQA Logo Design Guidelines

## Overview
The goal is to refresh the current OpenQA logo by making it look **glossier, shinier, and more premium**, while maintaining its existing single-color identity. We want to avoid complex multi-color gradients or changing the text color to black.

## Design Direction
- **Keep**: The current vibrant violet color (`#8b5cf6`).
- **Change**: The finish/texture. It currently looks "dull". It needs to look "shiny", "glossy", or "3D".
- **Avoid**: Complex gradients across the entire word, black text, or completely changing the logo shape.

## AI Generation Prompts

Use these prompts to "polish" the existing logo concept.

### Prompt 1: Glossy & 3D (The "Shine" Update)
> **Prompt:** A stunning, high-quality logo for "OpenQA". The design matches the existing logo but with a **premium glossy finish**. The text and icon are in a vibrant **electric violet** (`#8b5cf6`). The surface looks like **polished glass or liquid metal**, catching the light with subtle white highlights. It looks 3D and tactile, popping off the white background. Clean, modern, minimalist, but shiny. --v 6.0

### Prompt 2: Liquid Violet
> **Prompt:** The text "OpenQA" written in **liquid violet metal**. It has a high-gloss reflection, looking wet and shiny. The color is a rich, vibrant purple. White background. 8k resolution, product render style.

### Prompt 3: Glassmorphism Update
> **Prompt:** A logo for "OpenQA" made of **frosted and clear violet glass**. It glows slightly and has sharp, glossy edges. The aesthetic is modern and tech-focused. Single color (violet), but with depth and shine.

## Refinement Instructions
When refining the logo with AI:
1.  **Input**: Use the current flat logo as an image reference if possible.
2.  **Instruction**: "Make this look glossy and shiny. Keep the same color. Do not change the font significantly."
3.  **Key Adjectives**: Glossy, Shiny, Polished, Reflective, 3D, Premium.
